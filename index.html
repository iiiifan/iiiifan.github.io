About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), working in the Cognitive Science Laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Dewen Hu and Prof. Ling-Li Zeng. My research focuses on Brain Science & Cognitive Neuroscience and Brain-Computer Interface technologies.

I completed my M.Phil. degree in Control Science and Engineering at NUDT supervised by Prof. Dewen Hu and received my Bachelor's degree in Information Security from Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT).

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of neuroscience and artificial intelligence, focusing on:

  • EEG Signal Processing: Advanced deep learning methods for EEG analysis
  • Brain-Computer Interface: Non-invasive closed-loop BCI systems
  • Cognitive Neuroscience: Brain state monitoring and cognitive load prediction
